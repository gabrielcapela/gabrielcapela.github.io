Brazilian data scientist focused on implementing machine learning solutions to create value and support decision-making.

| End-to-End Machine Learning Solutions | Predictive Modeling | Data Analysis & Visualization |
| Dashboards | Automation | Decision Support Tools | Optimization & OR |

ACADEMIC BACKGROUND

  • Bachelor's Degree in Naval Sciences with an emphasis in Electronics
    Escola Naval (Brazilian Naval Academy)
  • Postgraduate Certificate in Operations and Logistics Management
    Universidade Federal do Rio Grande (FURG)
  • Current Undergraduate Degree in Data Science
    Universidade Federal de Mato Grosso do Sul (UFMS)

Experience

  • ➛ JUN 2024 – Currently: Freelance data scientist delivering real-world solutions through supervised learning, dashboards, and automation tools.
  • ➛ APR 2023 – SEP 2023: Led a data analysis project for the Personnel Command of the Brazilian Marine Corps, supporting strategic decisions that impacted over 15,000 service members.
  • ➛ JUN 2023 – AUG 2023: Developed a Python script to support the mathematical modeling of a multi-criteria decision-making method for an academic researcher.
  • ➛ FEB 2023 – Currently: Chose Data Science as a career focus and began contributing to scientific research. Since then, co-authored several papers published in respected international journals.
  • ➛ JUN 2022 – JAN 2023: Started studying Python with the ambition of building practical, technology-driven solutions.
