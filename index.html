Hi! I'm Gabriel Capela

Data Scientist | Machine Learning | Operational Researcher

Currently, I work as a freelance data scientist, delivering real-world solutions using machine learning techniques and data visualization to drive value for clients and partners.

In 2022, I started studying programming with Python, driven by the desire to create technology-based solutions. In 2023, I developed a Python script to support the mathematical modeling of a multi-criteria decision-making method for an academic researcher. That same year, I conducted a data analysis project for the General Command of the Brazilian Marine Corps, supporting decisions that impacted approximately 15,000 service members.

I actively contribute to a research group that integrates Machine Learning and Operations Research, participating in studies related to logistics, optimization, and decision support systems. I have also co-authored scientific articles published in academic journals and conferences.

I combine Data Science, Machine Learning, and Operations Research to support decision-making.

Academic Background

  • Graduate Degree in Naval Sciences with an emphasis in Electronics – Escola Naval (Brazilian Naval Academy)
  • Postgraduate Certificate in Operations and Logistics Management – Universidade Federal do Rio Grande (FURG)
  • Current Bachelor's Degree in Data Science – Universidade Federal de Mato Grosso do Sul (UFMS)

Experience

Since 2023, I’ve been working independently as a freelance data scientist, creating real-world solutions using supervised learning, automation, and dashboards.

My first professional project involved modeling a multi-criteria decision-making method for academic research. Later that year, I led a data analysis project for the General Command of the Brazilian Marine Corps, directly supporting strategic decisions affecting over 15,000 personnel.

I also collaborate with an interdisciplinary research group focused on combining Machine Learning with Operations Research. Our studies cover logistics optimization, algorithmic decision support, and data-driven strategy, with peer-reviewed publications presented in academic venues.
